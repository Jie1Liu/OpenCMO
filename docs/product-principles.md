# Product Principles

AIMO is a prototype AI marketing workspace, not a spam bot.

- AI may discover, analyze, score, draft, summarize, and recommend.
- Humans must approve external outbound messages.
- A reviewed reply may be sent only after a deliberate per-message user action.
- There is no bulk send, background auto-send, or private-message outreach.
- Mock channels must be visibly labeled; the competition demo uses live Bluesky public data.
